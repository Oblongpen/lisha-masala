Chicken Curry Preparation
Ingredients

Chilli, Turmeric, Coriander, Natural Spices.

Method
  1. Marinate 250g chicken with 20g curd and 1 packet of Lisha Chicken Curry Masala. Leave for 15 minutes.
  2. Make a paste of 50g onion, 50g tomato, and 4 green chillis.
  3. Saute sliced onion (50g) and curry leaves in edible oil. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry till golden brown.
  4. Add the onion-tomato paste and the marinated chicken. Mix well.
  5. Add 50g coconut milk and water as needed. Adjust salt and cook until tender. Garnish with coriander leaves.
Chicken Kabab Preparation
Usage

Perfect for Chicken Fry and Gobi Manchurian.

Method
  1. Clean chicken pieces thoroughly.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and Lisha Kabab Masala to the chicken. Mix well.
  3. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. Deep fry in hot oil on medium flame until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with raw onion rings and lemon wedges.
Fish Fry Preparation
Note

No Need to Add Salt

Method
  1. Clean the fish slices properly.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and Lisha Fish Fry Masala. Coat the fish evenly.
  3. Marinate for 30 minutes to let the flavors absorb.
  4. Deep fry in hot oil on medium flame until crispy and golden brown.
  5. Serve with onion and lemon.
Garam Masala Usage
Ingredients

Coriander, Aniseed, Chilli, Cassia, Garlic, Clove, Star Anise, Pepper, Dry Ginger, Cumin, Cardamom, Nutmeg, Curry Leaf.

Tip

This is a premium mixture of aromatic spices. Adding just 5g to 10g of Lisha Garam Masala will significantly enhance the taste and aroma of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.

Meat/Mutton Curry Preparation
Method
  1. Marinate 250g mutton with 20g curd and 50ml packet of Lisha Meat Curry Masala. Leave for 15 minutes.
  2. Make a paste of 50g onion, 50g tomato, and 4 green chillis.
  3. Saute sliced onion, curry leaves, and ginger-garlic paste in oil until golden brown.
  4. Add the prepared paste and marinated mutton. Mix well.
  5. Add 50g coconut milk, water, and salt to taste.
  6. Cook for 30 minutes until meat is tender. Garnish with coriander leaves.
